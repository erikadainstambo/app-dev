# Howl's Moving Castle

## Overview
**Title:** Howl's Moving Castle  
**Director:** Hayao Miyazaki  
**Studio:** Studio Ghibli  
**Release Year:** 2004  
**Genre:** Fantasy, Adventure, Romance, Animation  

---

## Plot Summary
*Howl's Moving Castle* follows **Sophie**, a young woman cursed by a witch and transformed into an old lady.  
Seeking to break the spell, she encounters **Howl**, a mysterious wizard who lives in a magical, walking castle powered by a fire demon named **Calcifer**.  

As Sophie learns more about Howl’s heart and courage, she discovers that love and kindness can overcome even the darkest magic.

---

## Main Characters
- **Sophie Hatter** – The brave and humble heroine.  
- **Howl Jenkins Pendragon** – A charming but secretive wizard.  
- **Calcifer** – A talking fire demon who powers the moving castle.  
- **Markl** – Howl’s loyal young apprentice.  
- **Witch of the Waste** – The one who curses Sophie.
